Licensing & Commissioning

SMR licensing and commissioning are conducted by national nuclear regulators to verify compliance with safety and operational standards. This oversight ensures that the unique modular designs and passive safety features of SMRs meet rigorous international nuclear-grade requirements.

The process includes design certification, combined construction and operating approval, and comprehensive testing. This phase focuses heavily on validating passive safety systems, reactor protection logic, and digital instrumentation and control (I&C) systems to guarantee a safe and predictable performance before official operation begins.
