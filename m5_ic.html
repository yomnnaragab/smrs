Instrumentation & Control Systems

SMR instrumentation and control systems are fully digital and factory-tested under extensive fault and operating scenarios. This rigorous testing ensures that the system can handle a wide variety of real-world conditions before it ever leaves the factory.

Once validated, the software is finalized and delivered as an integrated control module. This represents a significant advancement over traditional analog nuclear control systems, offering enhanced precision, automated safety logic, and cyber-secure architectures.
